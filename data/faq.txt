# WIZARD STORE - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

## ORDERING & PAYMENT

### Q1: How do I place an order?
Browse our product catalog, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, enter your shipping information, and complete payment. You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately after purchase.

### Q2: Can I modify my order after placing it?
Orders can only be modified within 2 hours of placement by contacting support@wizardstore.com. After that, the order enters our processing system and cannot be changed. You would need to place a new order and return the original items.

### Q3: Do you offer gift wrapping?
Yes! Premium gift wrapping is available for $7.99 per item. Select this option during checkout. We use themed wizard paper, elegant ribbons, and include a gift tag with your personalized message.

### Q4: Can I use multiple discount codes on one order?
No, only one discount code can be applied per order. The system will automatically apply the code that gives you the greatest savings.

### Q5: My discount code isn't working. What should I do?
Check that: (1) Code is still valid and not expired, (2) Items in cart are eligible (some limited editions are excluded), (3) You've met minimum purchase requirements if any, (4) Code is entered exactly as shown (case-sensitive). If issues persist, contact our support team.

### Q6: What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. All transactions are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway. We never store your credit card information on our servers.

### Q7: Can I cancel my order after purchase?
Orders can be cancelled within **2 hours** of placement by emailing support@wizardstore.com. If your order has already begun processing, cancellation may not be possible. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled, but you may refuse delivery or use our standard return policy.

## SHIPPING & DELIVERY

### Q8: How long does shipping take?
**Domestic:** 5-7 business days for standard shipping, 2-3 days for expedited, 1-2 days for express.  
**International:** 7-14 days (Canada), 10-21 days (UK/Europe), 14-28 days (Australia/NZ), varies elsewhere.  
Processing time is 1-2 business days before shipment.

### Q9: Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs: Canada ($15.99), UK & Europe ($24.99), Australia & New Zealand ($29.99), Rest of World ($34.99). Note: customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, VAT, or import fees charged by their destination country.

### Q10: Can I track my order?
Yes! Once your order ships (typically 1-2 business days), you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website and viewing order history.

### Q11: What if my package is lost or stolen?
Contact us immediately. For confirmed lost packages, we will file a claim with the carrier. Once approved, we'll send a replacement or issue a full refund. We recommend filing a police report for stolen packages to expedite the claims process.

### Q12: Do you ship to PO Boxes?
We ship most items to PO Boxes, but large items (robes, collector's trunks) and fragile collectibles may require a physical address. This will be indicated on the product page or you'll be notified during checkout.

## PRODUCTS & AVAILABILITY

### Q13: Are your products officially licensed?
Many of our products are officially licensed merchandise, while others are inspired designs and handcrafted replicas. Each product description indicates licensing status. We stand behind the quality and authenticity of all items we sell.

### Q14: What if an item I want is out of stock?
You can sign up for back-in-stock notifications on the product page. We'll email you when the item is available again. For limited edition items, we may not restock, so we recommend checking our "Limited Editions" section regularly.

### Q15: Can I request custom engravings or personalization?
Yes! Select wands and jewelry items offer custom engraving. Look for the "Personalization Available" option on product pages. Custom items take 3-5 additional business days to process and cannot be returned.

### Q16: What materials are the wands made from?
Our wands are made from various materials depending on the price point:
- Budget wands ($25-40): Durable resin with painted finish
- Mid-range wands ($70-100): Premium resin or composite wood with detailed crafting
- Premium wands ($120-200): Real wood (where specified), hand-carved, with luxury finishes
- Limited edition wands ($300+): Exotic woods, precious metal accents, display cases

### Q17: Are wands handcrafted?
Premium and limited edition wands ($120+) are hand-carved and hand-finished by skilled craftspeople. Mid-range wands feature hand-painted details. Budget wands are machine-made but still quality-inspected. Each product description specifies the level of craftsmanship.

### Q18: What sizes do your robes come in?
Our robes are available in sizes XS through XXL. Please refer to our detailed size chart on each product page. Robes are designed to fit loosely over regular clothing. If between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort and authentic draping.

## RETURNS & EXCHANGES

### Q21: What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for items in original, unused condition with all tags and packaging. Customer pays return shipping unless item is defective. Some items (personalized, opened stationery, limited editions with broken seals) cannot be returned. See our full Return Policy for details.

### Q22: How do I return an item?
Email support@wizardstore.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a Return Authorization (RA) number and instructions within 24 hours. Ship the item back with the RA number included. We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days after receiving and inspecting the return.

### Q23: Can I exchange an item for a different size or house color?
We don't offer direct exchanges. Please return the original item and place a new order for the size/color you need. This ensures you receive the new item as quickly as possible without waiting for the return to process.

### Q24: What if I received a damaged or defective item?
Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage or defect. We'll either send a replacement immediately or provide a prepaid return label for a full refund (including original shipping costs). We stand behind our products 100%.

## COLLECTIBLES & LIMITED EDITIONS

### Q29: What makes an item "limited edition"?
Limited edition items are produced in restricted quantities (typically 1,000-5,000 pieces) and include numbered certificates of authenticity. Once sold out, they are not restocked. These items often feature premium materials, special packaging, and exclusive designs.

### Q30: Are limited editions restocked?
No. Limited edition items are produced in specific quantities and once sold out, they are never restocked. This is what makes them collectible. We recommend purchasing limited editions when you see them, as they may not be available again. You can sign up for launch notifications for upcoming limited releases.

### Q31: Can limited edition items be returned?
Limited edition items with broken seals or removed from original packaging cannot be returned due to their collectible nature and numbered authenticity. Unopened limited editions may be returned within 30 days. Defective limited editions can always be returned for replacement or refund.

### Q32: How do I know if a limited edition item is authentic?
All our limited edition items include:
- Numbered certificate of authenticity
- Holographic authenticity sticker
- Premium packaging with item-specific details
- Registration card (for items over $200)

### Q33: Do collectibles increase in value?
While we can't guarantee investment returns, historically our limited edition wands, house cup trophies, and founder statue sets have appreciated in value on secondary markets. We recommend purchasing items you genuinely love rather than solely for investment purposes.

## CARE & MAINTENANCE

### Q38: How do I clean my wizard robe?
Most robes are machine washable on gentle cycle with cold water. We recommend washing dark colors separately and air drying to prevent shrinkage. Premium robes with extensive embroidery should be dry cleaned. Check the care tag on your specific robe.

### Q39: How should I store my wand collection?
Keep wands away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures to prevent warping or fading. Display cases, wall mounts, or the included storage boxes are ideal. Avoid stacking wands directly on top of each other.

### Q40: Can I clean my jewelry items?
Use a soft, dry cloth for regular cleaning. For sterling silver items, use a silver polishing cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals, ultrasonic cleaners, or submerging items with enamel details in water. Remove jewelry before swimming or showering.
