# WIZARD STORE - STORE POLICIES

## SHIPPING POLICY

### Domestic Shipping (United States)
- **Standard Shipping (5-7 business days)**: $5.99 for orders under $50
- **Expedited Shipping (2-3 business days)**: $12.99
- **Express Shipping (1-2 business days)**: $24.99
- **FREE Standard Shipping**: Available on all orders over $50

All domestic orders are shipped via USPS, FedEx, or UPS depending on package size and destination. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships, typically within 1-2 business days of order placement.

### International Shipping
- **Canada**: $15.99 flat rate (7-14 business days)
- **United Kingdom & Europe**: $24.99 flat rate (10-21 business days)
- **Australia & New Zealand**: $29.99 flat rate (14-28 business days)
- **Rest of World**: $34.99 flat rate (varies by location)

**Important International Notes:**
- Customs duties, taxes, and brokerage fees are the responsibility of the customer
- International orders may experience delays due to customs processing
- We are not responsible for packages refused due to customs fees
- Tracking is provided but may be limited once package enters destination country

### Processing Time
- Most orders ship within 1-2 business days
- Custom or personalized items (wands with custom engraving, etc.) may take 3-5 business days
- Limited edition items ship within 2-3 business days due to special packaging
- Pre-order items will ship on or shortly after the release date specified on product page
- During holiday seasons (October-December), processing may take up to 3 business days

### Shipping Restrictions
We currently cannot ship to:
- PO Boxes for certain large items (robes, collector's trunks)
- APO/FPO addresses for fragile collectibles
- Addresses in countries with import restrictions on replica weapons (check local laws regarding wand replicas)

## RETURN & REFUND POLICY

### Return Window
We accept returns within **30 days** of delivery date for most items. Item must be in original, unused condition with all tags, packaging, and accessories included.

### Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned due to hygiene, customization, or collectible nature:
- **Stationery items** that have been opened or written in
- **Custom or personalized items** (engraved wands, custom robes with numbers/names)
- **Limited edition items** with broken seals or removed from original packaging
- **Jewelry items** with broken hygiene seals
- **Final sale items** (marked as such on product page)
- **Gift cards**

### Return Process
1. **Initiate Return**: Email support@wizardstore.com with your order number and reason for return
2. **Receive Authorization**: We'll send you a Return Authorization (RA) number and return instructions within 24 hours
3. **Ship Item Back**: Package item securely in original packaging (if possible) and include RA number
4. **Return Shipping**: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless item is defective or we made an error
5. **Inspection**: We inspect returned items within 2-3 business days of receipt
6. **Refund Issued**: Refund is processed to original payment method within 5-7 business days after inspection

### Return Shipping Address

1420 Magical Lane  
Salem, MA 01970  
USA

**Return Shipping Costs:**
- Customer pays return shipping for standard returns
- We provide prepaid label for defective items or shipping errors
- International customers: we recommend tracked shipping method for returns

### Refund Details
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Original shipping charges are non-refundable (unless we made an error)
- Restocking fee of 15% applies to opened collectible items in original packaging
- Refunds typically appear in your account within 5-7 business days after processing

## DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS POLICY

We take great care in packaging all orders, but occasionally items may arrive damaged during transit.

### If You Receive a Damaged Item
1. **Take Photos**: Photograph the damaged item and packaging immediately upon receipt
2. **Contact Us**: Email support@wizardstore.com within **48 hours** of delivery with:
   - Order number
   - Photos of damage
   - Description of the issue
3. **Resolution Options**: We will offer:
   - Full refund
   - Replacement item (if in stock)
   - Partial refund if you wish to keep item with minor damage

### Defective Items
If an item is defective (audio features not working, broken clasp, manufacturing defect):
1. Contact us within **30 days** of receipt
2. Provide photos and description of defect
3. We will send a prepaid return label
4. Choose: replacement or full refund (including original shipping)

**Note:** We inspect all collectibles and limited edition items before shipping. Defects are extremely rare but we stand behind our products 100%.

## INTERNATIONAL ORDERS POLICY

### Customs & Import Duties
- All international packages are marked with accurate customs declarations
- Customer is responsible for any customs duties, taxes, VAT, or import fees charged by destination country
- We cannot mark packages as "gift" or undervalue items as this violates international shipping regulations
- Customs processing times vary by country and are beyond our control

### Lost or Delayed International Packages
- International shipments may take longer than estimated due to customs
- If a package has not arrived within 45 days, contact us and we will file a trace
- We are not responsible for packages held or seized by customs
- We recommend purchasing shipping insurance for high-value international orders

### International Returns
- Customer pays return shipping from international addresses
- We recommend tracked/insured shipping method
- Refunds do not include original shipping costs or customs fees paid
- International returns may take 3-4 weeks to process due to shipping times
